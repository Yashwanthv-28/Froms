Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions
  • 1/4 cup sliced olives
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add the pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, and olives as toppings.
  7. Sprinkle dried oregano and basil on top.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven, slice, and enjoy!
