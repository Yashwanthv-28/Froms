Burger Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, cheese, and other desired toppings

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, mix together ground beef, egg, bread crumbs, onion, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Shape the mixture into burger patties.
  3. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium heat. Cook the patties for 5-7 minutes on each side or until fully cooked.
  4. Toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  5. Assemble the burgers with your favorite toppings.
  6. Enjoy!
